Flipkart Intro

Flipkart is one of the largest e-commerce companies in India, founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal, who are not related. The company's headquarters are in Bangalore, India, and it is now majority-owned by Walmart. Flipkart started as an online bookstore and has since expanded into a wide range of product categories, including electronics, fashion, home goods, and groceries.

Flipkart has played a significant role in shaping the e-commerce landscape in India, particularly with its focus on providing a wide selection of products at competitive prices. The company has invested heavily in technology and logistics infrastructure to ensure that customers receive their orders quickly and efficiently.

One of the key factors in Flipkart's success has been its ability to innovate and adapt to changing market conditions. For example, the company was one of the first e-commerce companies in India to offer cash on delivery as a payment option, which has been particularly popular in a country where many people do not have credit or debit cards.
